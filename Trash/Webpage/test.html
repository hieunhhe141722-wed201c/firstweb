Experience in FPT

First time i went to FPT University, my mind completely blown away by its beauty. From the bus station to school, Alpha towering just 200m away. When you get closer, first thing that interest you is one of the greenest building that earns Green Building Awards in 2014 by a VietNamese architect and other rewards. Keep going ahead is 30m road lead you straight to Beta Build where the biggest libbrary is located with thoundsans of book are stored in different majors and subjects. College student will spend most of their time study in Beta and Alpha and leave Gamma for highschool student. Gamma was the first building that we joint for first-year English training course but did not improve much until we made it to major.

LUK 1
- Well in this level you basicly be treated like a child because they will teach you from the beginning like how to pronoun correctly like a actual native speaker.
- You probably feels uncomfortable and nervous at first but when you get used to it, your words will be more flexible also your pronunciation be improved.
- Early stage always fun and very interest because events and dance parties will apear every week in your schedule. The purpose here is to make new friends and be more active this also good for your brains both mental and physical. So enjoy it!
LUK 2
- Higher level but yes you still have to learn about pronounciation with a unforgetable quote Hello and Smile.
- At this stage you will have to start talking like alot because teachers will check on you more seriously, also at the end of Level 1 and Level 2 they will automatically test your ability to speak with correct rhythm.
- But don't worry because its not that difficult and LUK really know how to chill you down then heat you up, trust me i've been in your position once. feel free and relax a little bit, its not that hard if you promise to pratice daily :D
LUK 3
- Level 3 is a whole different level compare to 1 and 2 because your class will be seperate into teams for Presentation Events. This gives teachers oppotunities to justifie either you qualified or not.
- They'll start to teach your real soft-skill like Communication, Teamwork, Critical Thinking, Problem Solving
- So my advice is to prepare your presentation the more consistent the better your perform and remember to pratice your pronounciation.
- Try not to use notes when standing in front of whole class, you could learn by heart if you can. And also new task for you is to take notes every day(English News)
LUK 4 and LUK 5
- Level 4 and 5 is the time for you to head forward and learn by listening to news, science researchs, most of them are related to Neuroscience. You will also have activities to exercise your brains(you'll find out later when you reach level 4 and 5)
- This stage is very important because your pronounciation now have to be good because they will test you individualy through Video Essay with Fixed Contents that LUK provides. Quality is now matter, its not about the quality of your video, is the contents that you contribute in your video, the closer the content is the higher point you will get. So be aware and be consistent.
- Now the most interesting part DEBATE, some of you may heard about this but do you know how it actually works? Well I will tell you in my next part. Alot of fighting will happen during the Debate, not muscle to muscle, its ideas versus ideas, which worth widely spread and which will get higher vote. My review is done, see you in Debate zone.
